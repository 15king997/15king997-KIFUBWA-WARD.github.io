HOTELS NEARBY

The Royal Solwezi Hotel was officially opened by the former President - the Honourable Rupiah Banda - on the 27th August 2007. The Hotel is currently the most prestigious in the North West Province offering luxury amenities and easy access to the central business district. Now in its 11th year, the Royal Solwezi Hotel has hosted countless dignitaries, performers and exhibitors. Expect high levels of service, impressive facilities and dishes to suit any taste. With meeting rooms and conference space for up to 250 people, the Royal Solwezi Hotel is one of the most established conference hotels in Solwezi. The Hotel is located just 10km from First Quantum’s Kansanshi Mine, 60km from Barrick’s Lumwana Mine, and about a 15 minute drive from Solwezi Airportin Zambia. Stay connected with complimentary WiFi access, and work efficiently in the Business Centre. Guests can enjoy complimentary breakfast and refreshments overlooking the manicured gardens, while the outdoor swimming pool offers an ideal place to unwind after a busy day. Browse our website and let us know how our staff can help you make your stay in Zambia a memorable one. We look forward to welcoming you to Solwezi.

Kansanshi Hotel is an eco-friendly thatched hotel located on the beautiful quiet edge of Solwezi overlooking Savannah forest. We offer our guests a friendly African experience and panoramic views from our 34 luxurious rooms. The Lappa is ideal for all kinds of functions – from workshops, meetings and conferences to weddings and parties. Measuring 40 by 20 meters, it is the largest timber structure in Southern Africa. It houses the reception, bar, restaurant, two meeting rooms, deck and kitchen. The bar beside the swimming pool is also used for functions and children can play happily in our playground. Our chefs offer an international à la carte menu and buffet banquets tailored to parties and weddings. We also make cakes and cupcakes for your special occasions and celebrations. At Kansanshi Hotel, you will enjoy a good night’s sleep, high quality food, and our warm and our friendly service!
